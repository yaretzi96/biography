OAXACA

Your destination this summer

Located in the southern country of Mexico.Oaxaca is a dry and arid place with beautiful landscape. This beautiful southern state of Mexico, is full of hidden treasures, from small magical towns to its most visited beaches.The people of the state of Oaxaca are full of joy and are inviting to their visitors.Their richness in culture and people makes you get lost in the charm. Come and learn more about this rich cultural experience.

How to pack for your trip

To visit this marvelous place, you must consider its weather changes, its cold at sundown and hot on midday. Therefore make sure to pack appropiately.

You must pack:

  • Light clothing
  • Sweatters/Jackets
  • Sandles
  • Sneakers for long walks
